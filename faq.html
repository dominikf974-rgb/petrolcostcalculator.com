FAQ

Quick answers to the most common questions.
How is fuel consumption measured?

Most countries use L/100 km. The calculator multiplies your distance by your consumption and then by the price per litre.

Where can I find current fuel prices?

You can enter the price manually, or use the average shown on the page (if available for your region).

What are “running costs”?

Running costs are an estimate that can include maintenance, tyres, depreciation, insurance, taxes, and tolls (depending on your setup).

How do I split costs among passengers?

Enter the number of passengers (including the driver). The tool shows Total and Per Person.

Why does it say “Country not recognised”?

That means the auto-detection couldn’t match your input. Try “City, Country” (example: Prague, Czechia) or enter the fuel price manually.
