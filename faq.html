Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the fuel prices?

Prices are averages for each country and refreshed periodically. They are intended as guidance, not live pump data. For exact rates, consult local price services.

Can I override prices?

Yes. Enter your own price per litre and the calculator will use it for all computations until you reset to defaults.

Do you track my trips?

No. Calculations run in your browser. We do not store individual trip inputs server-side.

Which countries are supported?

Coverage includes major regions worldwide with default prices and city detection for many capitals. If you need an addition, email us and we will expand the list.

How are tolls estimated?

Tolls are approximated per 100 km by region, based on typical motorway fees. Actual costs vary by route, vehicle class, and local rules.

Do you provide an API?

We currently focus on the web tool. If you need API access or data sharing, contact us to discuss partnership options.

How can I report an issue?

Send details and screenshots to info@spritkosten-check.de. We prioritize fixes that impact correctness, accessibility, or security.
