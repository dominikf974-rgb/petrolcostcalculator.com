How to calculate fuel costs & cost per person

For quick estimates, you only need distance (km), consumption (L/100 km) and fuel price ($/L). The calculator also shows average costs per 100 km and the split per person.

Quick formulas

  • Litres = km √ó consumption √∑ 100
  • Fuel cost = litres √ó price
  • Per person = total cost √∑ people

Notes

Tips: save fuel & reduce travel costs

Small changes can reduce costs quickly: correct tyre pressure, steady speed, and realistic consumption values. If you travel together, split costs fairly per person.

  • Drive smoothly: accelerate and brake less aggressively.
  • Check tyre pressure: low pressure increases consumption.
  • Reduce weight: remove unnecessary load; use a roof box only if needed.
  • Split costs: use ‚ÄúPeople‚Äù for a fair per-person amount.
