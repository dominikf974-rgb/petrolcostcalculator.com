Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use Fuel Cost Calculator. We keep data collection minimal and transparent.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not require accounts. We do not store your individual trip calculations server-side. Inputs generally remain in your browser unless you share them yourself.

Data We May Process

  • Basic server logs for security and performance (IP address, user agent, timestamp).
  • Anonymous analytics to understand aggregate usage patterns and improve the site.
  • Geocoding requests sent to providers like Nominatim or Mapbox if you trigger map lookups.

Cookies and Storage

The site aims to minimize cookies. Some third-party services (e.g., map tiles, ads) may set their own cookies under their policies. You can block or clear cookies via your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

Geocoding and map tiles are provided by external services. Their processing of your requests is governed by their own privacy terms. We do not control their retention practices.

Data Security

We use HTTPS and reasonable safeguards to protect data in transit. No system is perfectly secure; avoid submitting sensitive personal data through the site.

Your Choices

You can refrain from using location inputs if you prefer not to share queries with geocoding providers. You may also use browser settings or extensions to limit tracking.

Data Retention

Server logs are retained for a limited period for security and troubleshooting, then purged. Analytics data is retained in aggregate form only.

International Use

The service is operated from Germany. If you use it from other regions, your data may be processed in or routed through the EU and other jurisdictions.

Contact

For privacy questions, reach us at info@spritkosten-check.de. We will address reasonable requests in line with applicable law.

Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be reflected by a revised effective date.
