Privacy Policy

← Back to the calculator

This Privacy Policy explains how we process information when you use PetrolCostCalculator.com. We keep data collection minimal and focus on transparency.

Data we do not intentionally store

We do not require accounts. We do not intentionally store your individual trip inputs (distance, consumption, price) as a user profile. Calculations primarily run in your browser.

Data we may process

  • Server logs (e.g., IP address, user agent, timestamp) for security and troubleshooting.
  • Map/geocoding requests if you use location search (sent to providers such as Nominatim/Mapbox).
  • Advertising (AdSense) which may use cookies/identifiers to serve and measure ads.
Important: Third-party providers have their own privacy policies. We do not control their retention.

Cookies

We aim to minimize cookies. However, third parties (ads, map tiles, geocoding) may set cookies under their own rules. You can manage cookies in your browser settings.

Data security

We use HTTPS and reasonable safeguards to protect data in transit. No method is 100% secure, so please avoid submitting sensitive personal data.

Retention

Server logs are kept for a limited time for security and then removed. We do not sell personal data.

For privacy questions: info@spritkosten-check.de
