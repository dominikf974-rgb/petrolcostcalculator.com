Privacy Policy

We take the protection of your personal data seriously. This policy explains how Spritkosten-Check (Fuel Cost Calculator) processes data when you use the website and tools.

1. Controller

Primary contact: info@spritkosten-check.de

2. Data processing overview

2.1 Scope

We process personal data only as needed to provide a functional website and the calculator. Most calculations run locally in your browser; no registration is required.

2.2 Legal bases

Depending on context, processing is based on consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR), contract performance or pre-contractual steps (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR), legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR), or legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) such as security, fraud prevention, and service stability.

2.3 Storage and deletion

We retain data only as long as necessary for the stated purposes or statutory retention. When the purpose ceases, data is erased or anonymised unless legal retention applies.

3. Website access and log files

When you access the site, the hosting system may log: IP address, date/time, requested path, referrer, user agent, and basic device/OS info. Log files are used to maintain security and stability and are deleted or anonymised routinely.

4. Fuel Cost Calculator

Inputs like distance, fuel type, consumption, price, and passengers are processed in your browser for calculation. By default, these values are not sent to our server. If you use the fuel price proxy, the request includes coordinates to retrieve local averages; see Section 5.

5. Fuel price proxy

When enabled, the calculator may call our proxy at http://localhost:3000/prices (or a configured endpoint) with latitude/longitude to fetch average prices. The proxy may query third-party sources (e.g., FuelWatch WA). Data used: coordinates, optional radius, and fuel type mapping. Responses include averaged prices; we do not build user profiles.

6. Cookies

The site aims to operate with minimal or no cookies. If cookies are set (e.g., remembering preferences), they serve usability. You can block or delete cookies in your browser; some functions may degrade.

7. Contact

If you email us, we process your email address and message content to handle the request. Data is deleted when the inquiry is resolved unless legal retention applies.

8. Data security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures (e.g., TLS where hosted) to protect data against unauthorised access, alteration, or loss.

9. Your rights (GDPR)

  • Access, rectification, erasure
  • Restriction and objection to processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time (does not affect prior processing)
  • Complain to a supervisory authority

10. International aspects

Servers or third-party APIs may reside outside your country. We aim to use providers with appropriate safeguards (e.g., standard contractual clauses) where applicable.

11. Changes

We may update this policy to reflect legal or technical changes. The latest version is available on this page.
