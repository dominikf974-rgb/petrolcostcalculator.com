Tips: Save fuel & reduce trip costs

Small changes can cut costs quickly: correct tyre pressure, steady speeds, and realistic consumption figures. If you’re travelling together, split costs fairly per person.

  • Drive smoothly: avoid hard acceleration and braking.
  • Check tyre pressure: low pressure increases consumption.
  • Reduce weight: remove unnecessary load, use a roof box only when needed.
  • Split costs: use “People” for a fair per-person amount.
