Quick FAQ

Does this work for villages and small towns?

Yes. We use OpenStreetMap geocoding and reverse-geocoding, so small towns and villages are detected as well.

Can I enter my own fuel price?

Yes. Type your price anytime. Click “Reset to default” to restore the detected country defaults.

How accurate are toll estimates?

Tolls are an estimate based on region sampling. Actual costs depend on route, vehicle class and local rules.

How is running cost calculated?

Running costs include a simple estimate for maintenance/depreciation plus the toll estimate per 100 km.
