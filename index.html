Fuel Cost Calculator - FAQ

How is fuel consumption measured?

Fuel consumption is measured in litres per 100 kilometres (L/100 km). If your car uses 7.5 litres per 100 km, enter 7.5.

Where can I find current fuel prices?

The calculator uses average prices for your detected country. You can always override the price manually.

How accurate are the toll cost estimates?

Toll estimates are based on average motorway fees and vignettes per country. Actual tolls depend on route and vehicle class.
