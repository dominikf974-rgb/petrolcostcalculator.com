Fuel cost per 100 km

Cost per 100 km is the best metric to compare cars and routes — because the fuel price is included. For a quick result you can also use the calculator.

Formula

Fuel cost per 100 km = consumption (L/100 km) × price per litre

Example: 6.8 L/100 km × €1.85 = €12.58 per 100 km

Example calculation

  • Consumption: 7.2 L/100 km
  • Price: €1.80/L

7.2 × 1.80 = €12.96 per 100 km

Convert to cost per km and trip cost

Cost per km = (cost per 100 km) ÷ 100

Example: €12.96 per 100 km → 12.96 ÷ 100 = €0.1296/km. More details: Cost per km.

Trip cost = km × cost per km

FAQ

How do I calculate cost per 100 km?
Consumption × price.

How do I convert cost per 100 km to cost per km?
Divide by 100.

What is it useful for?
Real comparisons between cars and routes — faster than “litres only”.
